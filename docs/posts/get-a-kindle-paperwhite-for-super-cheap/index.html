Get a Kindle Paperwhite for super cheap

2016-11-19 — Written by Brian Smith

Black Friday or not

Several years ago I got a Nook Simple Touch. I love the e-ink screen and the simplicity of a device focused solely on reading. No disruptive notifications or games, just books.

Although the screen was nice, I always felt like the ecosystem was lacking. I own a lot of books on Kindle, and the Kindle app no longer works on the dated Nook simple touch. I’ve also often wished the Nook had a built in light for the screen, for night reading.

Curious, I looked into buying a Kindle Paperwhite. I was shocked to see the going price for a new one was $120, plus another $20 just to remove ads!

New Kindle Paperwhite on Amazon, $119.99

Luckily, there’s a cheaper option. After doing some research, I found that Amazon itself sells the previous model used at a steep discount. The only difference between the two models is that the new one has slightly more storage, slightly more pixels, and slightly less battery life.

Used Kindle Paperwhite on Amazon, $50.49

Click on the “used” section of the above link to see the amazon warehouse deals:

That’s $50.49 for a good condition Kindle Paperwhite with the ads already removed. That’s 65% off just for buying used! The one they sent me doesn’t look used at all though, it’s perfect!

Hard to believe it’s not a book, right?

In the end, you’ll just have to decide for yourself whether or not it’s worth the $90 premium to get a slightly newer device with slightly less battery life.

I’m happy with the used one.
